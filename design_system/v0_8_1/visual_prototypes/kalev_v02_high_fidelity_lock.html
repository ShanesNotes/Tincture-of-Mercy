§ 01The man, as drawn

CANONICAL · kalev_concept_foundation_v0_3.png · 1122 × 1402

This is Kalev. Long weathered cedar coat over a charcoal wool layer; an asymmetric scarf at the throat in cold blue-charcoal; a leather-bound notebook held in his left hand, a medicine pouch at his right hip, the cedar dog charm hanging just below it on a short cord; a small Ember vial tucked against the pouch with a single muted red accent; one fingerless glove on the right hand; a faded clinical armband — a single linen-bandage cross on the upper-right sleeve — the only echo of the hospital on him. Boots heavy and practical. Posture forward, shoulders carrying weight.

He does not pose. He stands. The tired eyes and held mouth do all the emotional work; the costume is competent, layered, and entirely non-heroic. Read this concept as the model sheet for face, costume, material language, and accessory placement — and as the single strongest argument for moving Kalev off the 32 × 48 placeholder.

"Competent, exhausted, quiet, and dangerous only in the way a person can become dangerous when they try to outwork grief."

Below are the locks the runtime sprite must inherit, in priority order. The 64 × 96 redraw will preserve silhouette, accessory placement, palette family, and emotional register; it will not preserve concept-sheet detail-density. Pixel art is a translation, not a downscale.

§ 06Acceptance gates — KALEV-01 → KALEV-20

A build cannot call Kalev art-ready until all twenty pass. Grouped by what they fail when they fail.

Readability · KALEV-01 → 06

  • KALEV-01At 1×, silhouette reads as long coat, burdened shoulders, practical boots.
  • KALEV-02At 1×, Kalev does not read as warrior, rogue, paladin, or combat class.
  • KALEV-03At 1×, at least one of notebook or pouch is clearly readable.
  • KALEV-04At 2×, notebook, pouch, cedar dog, and Ember vial are legible.
  • KALEV-05Ember restrained: no persistent glow outside Ember frames.
  • KALEV-06No weapon silhouette in idle or walk frames.

Animation · KALEV-07 → 11

  • KALEV-07Idle has restrained breath / coat weight, not cozy bounce.
  • KALEV-08Walk feels practical and heavy, not heroic.
  • KALEV-09write_name makes the notebook action unmistakable.
  • KALEV-10administer_ember is tempting and clinical, not flashy magic.
  • KALEV-11Care animations exist or are scheduled before any action / combat.

Godot integration · KALEV-12 → 16

  • KALEV-1264 × 96 frames import nearest-neighbor, no smoothing.
  • KALEV-13Anchors correctly on 32 × 32 tile floors.
  • KALEV-14Collision shape covers body / feet, not coat silhouette.
  • KALEV-15Fits through Cabin / Bethany doors after collision tuning.
  • KALEV-16Remains readable across Cabin, Ironwood Road, Bethany palettes.

Canon / drift · KALEV-17 → 20

  • KALEV-17Cedar dog remains present but not oversized.
  • KALEV-18Notebook remains present; he is a name-bearer, not a generic traveler.
  • KALEV-19No attack / dodge / victory animation added for P0.
  • KALEV-20Concept sheet is not used directly as AnimatedSprite2D runtime art.
