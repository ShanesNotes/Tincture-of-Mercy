# Kalev Animation Priority v0.3

Kalev’s animation budget should express care before competence, and responsibility before power.

## P0 — required for playable slice

| Animation | Purpose | Frames | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| idle_4dir | player presence | 4 each | tiny breath, coat weight, no bounce |
| walk_4dir | exploration | 4 each | practical, heavy, legible on 32×32 tiles |
| write_name | notebook action | 3 | page opens, pencil lowers, graphite pause |
| administer_ember | temptation action | 5 | vial draw, red glint, tongue/drop/hand recoil |
| interact_reach | generic interaction | 2 | hand moves toward prop or patient |

## P1 — care verbs

| Animation | Purpose | Frames | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| sit | presence | 2 | mechanically meaningful stillness |
| wash | dignity care | 4 | cloth and basin motion, no purification magic |
| warm | blanket/compress | 3 | human warmth, not stat buff |
| kneel | bedside action | 3 | patient care and name writing bridge |

## P2 — polish

| Animation | Purpose | Frames | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| pressure_tremor | high Pressure overlay | shader/2 | cursor/hand wobble; accessibility-safe |
| numb_idle | post-Ember body state | 4 | cleaner, colder stillness; faces less attended |
| pouch_check | inventory affordance | 3 | tactile pouch handling |

## Anti-drift rule

No attack, dodge, weapon-ready, victory, or heroic flourish animations for the prototype.
