# Kalev Visual Locks v0.3

## Identity sentence

Kalev Ward is a former critical-care nurse moving through post-Turn Michigan as a burdened healer: competent, exhausted, quiet, and dangerous only in the way a person can become dangerous when they try to outwork grief.

## Silhouette locks

At gameplay scale, Kalev must read by silhouette before detail:

1. Long weathered coat or mantle.
2. Slight forward weight / burdened shoulders.
3. Dark scarf or cowl mass at neck.
4. Medicine pouch on right hip.
5. Notebook on left side or carried left-hand.
6. Practical heavy boots.
7. No weapon silhouette.

## Accessory locks

| Detail | Placement | Runtime readability |
|---|---|---|
| Leather notebook | Left hand / left side | Must read at 1× as a rectangular held object or attached book. |
| Medicine pouch | Right hip | Must read at 1× by silhouette, at 2× by flap/seam detail. |
| Cedar dog charm | Below pouch or strap | Visible at 2×; not oversized. |
| Ember vial | Near pouch / inner coat | Tiny muted red accent; visible at 2×; flashes only in Ember animation. |
| Old clinical trace | Armband, worn patch, or chart-band echo | Subtle; no modern hospital costume cosplay. |

## Face locks

Kalev is not handsome-hero polished. He is human, tired, and attentive.

- Short dark hair.
- Short beard or heavy stubble.
- Tired eyes.
- Mouth neutral or held shut.
- No smirk.
- No heroic glare.

## Palette locks

Use muted cedar brown, rot brown, ash gray, desaturated blue-charcoal, worn leather, faded linen, and tiny restrained Ember red.

Gold is not a costume color for Kalev. If gold appears, it is a sacred or false-armor disclosure in a specific beat, not a general outfit accent.

## Animation attitude

Kalev does not bounce like a cozy RPG villager. His idle breath is restrained. His walk is practical and slightly heavy. Care animations matter more than action animations.
