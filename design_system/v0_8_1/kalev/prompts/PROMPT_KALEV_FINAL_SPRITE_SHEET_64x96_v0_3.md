# Prompt — Kalev Final Runtime Sprite Sheet 64×96 v0.3

Use this for a human artist, internal art agent, or image-generation exploration. Do not ask for imitation of any living artist.

```text
Create the final runtime sprite sheet for Kalev Ward, the main character of Tincture of Mercy: The Garments of Skin.

Style: premium large-pixel 2D top-down / slight three-quarter gameplay sprite art for a Godot narrative survival-care game. The direction is Michigan icon-manuscript pixel art: damp post-Turn Michigan, Orthodox-apocalyptic manuscript restraint, practical healer materials, cold muted palette, strong silhouette, and clean pixel clusters.

Frame target: 64×96 pixels per frame, transparent PNG. Use a 5-column sheet layout. Do not include background, labels, grid lines, glow effects, or concept-sheet decoration.

Character: Kalev Ward, age 33, former critical-care nurse, burdened healer, not an action hero. He wears a long weathered cedar-brown coat with heavy scarf/collar mass, dark gloves, practical boots, layered cold-weather clothes, medicine pouch on right hip, leather notebook visible, small cedar dog charm, and a small restrained Ember vial as a red accent. He should read as tired, grave, competent, and human.

Silhouette locks: long coat, burdened shoulders, pouch, notebook, boots, scarf/collar. At 2×, show the cedar dog charm and Ember vial, but do not make them large or magical.

Animations:
Row 0 idle_down 4 frames
Row 1 idle_left 4 frames
Row 2 idle_up 4 frames
Row 3 idle_right 4 frames
Row 4 walk_down 4 frames
Row 5 walk_left 4 frames
Row 6 walk_up 4 frames
Row 7 walk_right 4 frames
Row 8 write_name 3 frames
Row 9 administer_ember 5 frames
Optional/P1 rows: wash 4, warm 3, sit 2, kneel 3.

Motion rules: no combat swagger, no heroic stance, no weapons. Walk should feel heavy but playable. Write-name should feel careful and intimate. Administer-Ember should feel precise and dangerous through hand posture and vial position, not through magic effects.

Palette: muted cedar browns, damp wool grays, blue-charcoal, worn leather, linen bone, tiny Ember red. Avoid bright fantasy saturation, neon, clean sci-fi medic colors, shiny armor, and cozy-village warmth.

Important: Do not downscale the concept sheet. Redraw the sprite sheet natively at 64×96 per frame. Maintain crisp pixel-art edges suitable for nearest-neighbor scaling in Godot.
```
