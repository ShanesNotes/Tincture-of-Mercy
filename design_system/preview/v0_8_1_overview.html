§ 5What v0.8.1 will not let you do

The contract has been hardened so the implementation does not invent canon.

— No combat. No farming. No hospital-management sim.
— No new ingredients in P0 (no Bitterleaf, no Arbor, no Acebark…).
— No new patients. No renamed patients (Nora Finch; Dr. Amos Bell).
— No Ember in slot 8. No item but cedar dog in slot 1.
— No Wittehaven, Mackinac, Paradise, Iron Ledger as playable.
— No write to page 77 line 7 in any prototype path.
— No IC XC NIKA, narthex, halos in P0 atlases.
— No JetBrains Mono. No music. No celebration sounds.
— No raw Pressure / Burden / Numbness numbers in player-facing UI.
— No ProgressBar for patient state. No morality meter.
— No Commit N+1 before Commit N's acceptance row passes.
