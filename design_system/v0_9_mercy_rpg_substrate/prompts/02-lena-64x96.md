## Prompt

```text
You are producing the runtime sprite sheet for Lena Hart, a Bethany companion in *Tincture of Mercy: The Garments of Skin* — a combat-capable mercy RPG vertical slice in Godot 4.6 / C# / Forward+. Authoring rules below are non-negotiable.

CHARACTER
- Lena Hart, principal-adult companion. Enters during Bethany triage in later phases; the recognition payoff for the boy Kalev protected in Act 5 lands in her room, not at her hands.
- Presence over instruction. Practical, weathered, kept. She does not carry the tincture; her authority is reading the room, not dispensing medicine.
- Silhouette laws (must read at 2× zoom):
  · stitched leather gloves with fingertip cut (visible knuckles and fingertips)
  · `pale-heal-deep` (#5A6B48) shawl draped on shoulders, falling to mid-back
  · linen apron over working dress, pockets visible
  · practical boots, lower-cuff than Kalev's
  · sleeves rolled to forearm; wrists visible
  · hair pulled back; small temple-piece visible at rest
  · upright, working posture — never burdened slump, never hero stance
- Avoid: gloves intact (must show repair); tincture vial; cedar dog; weapons; nurse uniform white; clinical mask; fantasy robes.

CANVAS
- 64×96 pixels per frame
- 5 columns × 9 rows = 320 px wide × 864 px tall, transparent PNG, RGBA
- No background, no grid lines, no labels, no guide rulers
- Feet baseline y = 84 across every frame

ANIMATION ROWS (matches v0.8.1 manifest §6.2 Lena entry)
  Row 0: idle_down,         4 frames + 1 empty cell — quiet vigilance, weight evenly placed
  Row 1: idle_left,         4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 2: idle_up,           4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 3: idle_right,        4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 4: walk_down,         4 frames + 1 empty — purposeful, never hurried
  Row 5: walk_left,         4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 6: walk_up,           4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 7: walk_right,        4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 8: hand_on_shoulder,  3 frames + 2 empty — slow placement, hold, release; the Bethany recognition gesture

PRIORITY-2 ROWS (defer to second pass after the 9-row sheet ships)
  prep_table_work 4 frames — at the bench preparing cloth/water/herb
  stitch_glove    4 frames — repairing the glove she wears; visible needle and thread
  sit             2 frames — bench/stool sit, hands on knees

PIXEL-ART RIGOR
- Author every pixel natively at 64×96. No upscale-and-clean, no downscale-and-process.
- One-pixel solid outline in ink (#1A1612). No anti-aliasing on silhouette. No semi-transparent silhouette pixels.
- ≤ 24 unique colors per frame; ≤ 48 unique colors over the whole sheet.
- Dither: 1px or 2px ordered only, on shawl folds and apron creases. Never spray noise.
- 12fps animation cadence; keyframes over tween smear.

PALETTE (use these tokens; do not introduce others)
- Shawl:               pale-heal-deep #5A6B48 (base) · pale-heal #7A8C5E (highlight) · pine-shadow #2A3329 (deep fold)
- Apron / linen:       vellum-warm #EFE6D0 (base) · damp-ash #6B6358 (shade) · candle-white #F6F1E0 (highlight)
- Working dress:       rot-brown #4A3A26 (base) · ink-soft #2B2520 (deep fold) · damp-ash #6B6358 (mid-tone)
- Gloves (repaired):   cedar-brown #6B4A2C (leather) · ink #1A1612 (stitch lines) · vellum-bone #E8DFC9 (exposed fingertip)
- Skin / face:         vellum-warm #EFE6D0 (highlight) · damp-ash #6B6358 (shade) · ink-soft #2B2520 (line)
- Hair:                rot-brown #4A3A26 (base) · ink #1A1612 (deep)
- Boots:               ink #1A1612

OUTPUT
- File: `art/characters/lena/lena_full_sheet_64x96.png`
- One transparent-PNG sprite sheet meeting every constraint above.
- Empty cells fully transparent (alpha 0, all channels).

ACCEPTANCE
1. Sheet is exactly 320 × 864 px (or 320 × 1152 px if priority-2 rows are included in the same pass).
2. Every frame's feet sit on y = 84 within its cell.
3. ≤ 48 unique colors over the whole sheet.
4. Silhouette is single-pixel outlined; no semi-transparent silhouette pixels.
5. At 1× pixel scale, a single 64×96 cell is recognizable as Lena: shawl, repaired gloves, apron, upright working posture. She must NOT be confused with Kalev — her silhouette is shorter-cloaked, lighter, and shawl-bearing rather than coat-bearing.
6. The three `hand_on_shoulder` frames carry presence through hand position alone — no halo, no light, no aura.

REFERENCES (silhouette and accessory guide ONLY; do not trace; do not downscale)

Do not imitate any living artist. Return one PNG.
```
