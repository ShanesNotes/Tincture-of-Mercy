## Prompt

```text
You are producing the runtime sprite sheet for the Boy, the protected child in *Tincture of Mercy: The Garments of Skin* — a combat-capable mercy RPG vertical slice in Godot 4.6 / C# / Forward+. Authoring rules below are non-negotiable.

CHARACTER
- Unnamed boy in the opening slice. His name is resolved at runtime through later notebook events; in Acts 1–5 he is the person Kalev tends, feeds, witnesses with, and protects. The future Bethany recognition payoff hinges on what he remembers from these acts.
- Apparent age 7–9. Smaller than minor adults (48×72) and taller than infants. The 48×64 canvas is narrower-than-tall because he is upright and slim.
- Silhouette laws (must read at 2× zoom):
  · oversized rot-brown hand-me-down coat to mid-thigh (sleeves slightly past hands)
  · one shoulder slightly raised — the carried-fear posture
  · linen scarf wound at the neck, ends tucked
  · woolen cap — small, tight to the head, not floppy
  · practical small boots, scuffed
  · hands usually clasped or held close to the body in idle frames
  · small visible breath puff in cold-Michigan frames (1–2 pixels of candle-white near the mouth)
- Avoid: smiling face, cherub features, fantasy waif, comic-relief energy, sword/weapon, oversized eyes, anime stylization. He is a real frightened child — not cute, not pitiful, present.

CANVAS
- 48×64 pixels per frame
- 5 columns × 9 rows = 240 px wide × 576 px tall, transparent PNG, RGBA
- No background, no grid lines, no labels, no guide rulers
- Feet baseline y = 56 across every frame

ANIMATION ROWS
  Row 0: idle_down,        4 frames + 1 empty cell — quiet, watchful; one shoulder raised
  Row 1: idle_left,        4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 2: idle_up,          4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 3: idle_right,       4 frames + 1 empty cell
  Row 4: walk_down,        4 frames + 1 empty — small steps, slightly behind a leading adult silhouette
  Row 5: walk_left,        4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 6: walk_up,          4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 7: walk_right,       4 frames + 1 empty
  Row 8: receive_bread,    3 frames + sit 2 (= 5 frames total)
    · receive_bread frames 1–3: hands extending → bread received → hands draw bread close to chest
    · sit frames 4–5: kneeling/sitting on heels with bread, head slightly bowed

PRIORITY-2 ROWS (defer to second pass after the 9-row sheet ships)
  hide_behind     2 frames — partially obscured behind Kalev's coat (right side of cell)
  advance_safety  4 frames — moving toward a route node, head looking back once across the cycle

PIXEL-ART RIGOR
- Author every pixel natively at 48×64. No upscale-and-clean, no downscale-and-process.
- One-pixel solid outline in ink (#1A1612). No anti-aliasing on silhouette. No semi-transparent silhouette pixels.
- ≤ 20 unique colors per frame; ≤ 36 unique colors over the whole sheet.
- Dither: 1px ordered only, on coat folds. Never spray noise.
- 12fps animation cadence; keyframes over tween smear. Idle holds 200ms+ on the resting frame.
- The breath puff (when included) is 1–2 candle-white pixels and fades over 2 frames; never a cloud, never a white burst.

PALETTE (use these tokens; do not introduce others)
- Coat (oversized hand-me-down): rot-brown #4A3A26 (base) · ink-soft #2B2520 (deep fold) · damp-ash #6B6358 (worn highlight)
- Scarf:                         pine-shadow #2A3329 (base) · damp-ash #6B6358 (shade)
- Cap:                           cedar-brown #6B4A2C (base) · ink #1A1612 (deep)
- Skin / face:                   vellum-warm #EFE6D0 (highlight) · damp-ash #6B6358 (shade) · ink-soft #2B2520 (line)
- Hair (visible under cap):      rot-brown #4A3A26 · ink #1A1612 (deep)
- Boots:                         ink #1A1612 (no second tone — keep them tight)
- Bread (in receive_bread row):  vellum-warm #EFE6D0 (base) · old-ochre #B08A4A (crust) · damp-ash #6B6358 (shadow)
- Optional cold-breath:          candle-white #F6F1E0, ≤ 2 pixels per frame

OUTPUT
- File: `art/characters/boy/boy_full_sheet_48x64.png`
- One transparent-PNG sprite sheet meeting every constraint above.
- Empty cells fully transparent (alpha 0, all channels).

ACCEPTANCE
1. Sheet is exactly 240 × 576 px (or 240 × 768 px if priority-2 rows are included in the same pass).
2. Every frame's feet sit on y = 56 within its cell.
3. ≤ 36 unique colors over the whole sheet.
4. Silhouette is single-pixel outlined; no semi-transparent silhouette pixels.
5. At 1× pixel scale, a single 48×64 cell is recognizable as a small frightened child in oversized coat and cap. He must NOT read as a small adult — proportions matter (head slightly larger, limbs slightly thinner).
6. The receive_bread sequence carries the Act 2 recognition-seed weight without sentimental overdraw — no smile, no glow, no halo. Hands and the angle of the head do the work.
7. The shoulder asymmetry (one shoulder raised) is visible in idle frames and must persist subtly through walks. It is the silhouette tell.

REFERENCES (silhouette guide ONLY; do not trace; do not downscale)
- `art/characters/birdie/birdie_design_asset.png` — Birdie concept reference (1122×1402; concept-only). Birdie is a v0.8.1 cast member; the v0.9 boy carries forward the silhouette laws (oversized coat, raised shoulder, quiet posture) but is not the same character. Use as silhouette guide, not as identity transfer.
- `design_system/v0_9_mercy_rpg_substrate/03-opening-act-bible.md` §Acts 2, 4, 5 — verbs, events, and recognition seeds the sprite has to support
- `design_system/v0_9_mercy_rpg_substrate/04-latent-paths-receptivity.md` — `bread_received`, `protected_on_road` recognition hooks
- `design_system/colors_and_type.css` — palette tokens

Do not imitate any living artist. Return one PNG.
```
