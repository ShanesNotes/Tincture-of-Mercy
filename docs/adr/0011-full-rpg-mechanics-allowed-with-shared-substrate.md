# Full RPG mechanics are allowed under the shared substrate

The active v0.9 direction allows normal RPG mechanics when coherent with Tincture's world: combat risk, damage, threat, timers, loot, item quality, rarity, equipment/materials, XP-style internal accounting, Witness, Recollection, Vocation/path points, death/friction, and authored gravity encounters.

The design constraint is not to soften combat or strip reward. The constraint is that combat, care, craft, economy, notebook, and aftermath share event truth and domain presenters.

Considered Options:
- Treat combat rewards as symbolic only: rejected because the active direction and WoW-shaped substrate explicitly support RPG risk/reward.
- Make loot the primary objective of the wolf encounter: rejected because protection and boy safety are the encounter objective.
- Allow full RPG mechanics with event-derived tuning: chosen because it preserves first-class combat while keeping mercy, care, and consequence readable through the same substrate.

Tuning levers such as scarcity, danger, diminishing returns, time pressure, and route changes remain available. They should be scoped to balance and world logic rather than framed as broad exclusions.
